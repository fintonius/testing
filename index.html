About

In the heart of an enchanted forest, the Mythical Creatures Rehabilitation Center stands as a timeless refuge for sentient beings in need of respite and care. Established in an era long past, our commitment to healing and renewal has never wavered. We invite all mythical creatures, from weary dragons to contemplative centaurs, to find solace in our embrace and embark on a journey of recovery and rediscovery.
